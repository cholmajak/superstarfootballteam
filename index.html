Our Story

The journey of Superstar Football Club began in 2012, born from the passion and determination of a group of students in Northern Uganda. Far from home, these students shared a common love for football and a dream to create a team that would one day become a symbol of hope and excellence.

What started as informal kickabouts quickly grew into a structured team. With resilience and a united spirit, they honed their skills and fostered a unique identity. This foundation, built on friendship and a shared vision, was carried back to Bor Town, South Sudan, where Superstar FC was officially established.

Today, the club stands as a testament to their founding vision, continuing to inspire young talent and represent the pride of our community on and off the pitch. We are more than a club; we are a community.
