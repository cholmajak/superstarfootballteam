Our Story & Legacy

The journey of Superstar Football Club began in 2012, born from the passion and determination of a group of students in Northern Uganda. Far from home, these students shared a common love for football and a dream to create a team that would one day become a symbol of hope and excellence.

What started as informal kickabouts quickly grew into a structured team. With resilience and a united spirit, they honed their skills and fostered a unique identity. This foundation, built on friendship and a shared vision, was carried back to Bor Town, South Sudan, where Superstar FC was officially established.

Today, the club stands as a testament to their founding vision, continuing to inspire young talent and represent the pride of our community on and off the pitch.

Our Home: Bor Stadium

Our home ground is the renowned Bor Stadium. It's more than just a pitch; it's our fortress, where countless battles have been won and where our community comes together to cheer us on. The electric atmosphere on match day is a testament to the passion for football in our town.

The Football Academy

We are committed to nurturing the next generation of superstars. Our football academy provides a structured program for young, aspiring players in Bor. We focus on technical skills, tactical awareness, and personal development, creating well-rounded athletes and individuals.

Training Sessions

Discipline and hard work are the cornerstones of our success. Our team holds rigorous training sessions focused on fitness, strategy, and teamwork. Under the guidance of our coaching staff, every session is an opportunity to improve and prepare for the next challenge.
